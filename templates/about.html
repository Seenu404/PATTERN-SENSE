About Pattern Sense

Pattern Sense is an innovative web application that classifies textile patterns using deep learning techniques. Powered by Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), it aims to simplify the identification of complex textile designs and support designers, researchers, and production teams with efficient pattern analysis.

Whether you're dealing with classic designs or modern aesthetics, Pattern Sense provides an intelligent solution to decode and understand the world of fabrics. Our mission is to blend technology with creativity to enhance textile innovation.
